The Causes, Effects and Solutions to "Climate Change"

Oct 29 2021 Edited by: Furnum

What is climate change?

Climate change is not just about the rise of the average of temperature, it also includes the extreme weather, the changes of the populations and habitats of wildlives, rising oceans and so on.

What causes climate change?

Most of these changes are occurring as humans keeping emitting heat-absorbing greenhouse gases too the atmosphere, one of them is well-known, CO2(Carbon Dioxide).

There exits other factors causing climate change, for example(Click texts or figures for more details.):

Volcanic eruptions
Variations in solar radiation from sunspots
Solar wind
Earth's position relative to the sun

Any solutions?

  • Reducing electricity generation from coal and natural gas.
  • Increase the amount of electricity coming from clean, renewable sources such as solar and wind.
  • Prevention of deforestation and tree felling.
  • Reforestation and afforestation.
  • Shop sustainably.
  • Call on our leaders to introduce stronger laws to stop the excessive cutting of trees.
